Isaac Davis Trail
On the day of the Concord celebrations, the Acton Minutemen usually walk the "Captain Isaac Davis Trail" (the road the Acton patriots took), which was mapped out many decades ago by some scouts from ACTON  TROOP 1 of the Boy Scouts.  Dozens of boy scout troops come each year for a Scout rally on the weekend of the reenactments held on land along Route 2 – in Acton.
